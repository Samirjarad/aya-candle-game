How to play

Tap falling candels to light them. Avoid tapping bombs. Tap gift boxes to add extra seconds.

  • Candels: +1 score
  • Bombs: -5 score
  • Gifts: +1/+2/+3 seconds

Tip: It’s easier if you tap quickly—speed increases as time passes.
